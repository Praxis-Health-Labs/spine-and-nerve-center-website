BE PREPARED

Important Documents for Your Personal Injury Visit

Please bring all relevant documentation to ensure we can provide comprehensive care and proper case documentation

Accident Information

  • Police report
  • Accident date & location
  • Insurance claim number
  • Photos of injuries/accident
  • Witness information

Legal Information

  • Attorney name & contact
  • Case number
  • Letter of Protection (if applicable)
  • PIP/Med Pay information
  • Authorization forms

Medical Documentation

  • ER/Hospital records
  • X-rays, MRIs, CT scans
  • Previous treatment records
  • Medication list
  • Prior medical history

Important Notice

Time is critical in personal injury cases. Florida PIP benefits require treatment within 14 days of your accident. Additionally, many diagnostic tests and treatments must be completed within specific timeframes to be valid for your case.

If you're missing any documents, don't delay your appointment. We can help you obtain necessary records and coordinate with your legal team.

BILLING & INSURANCE

We Handle the Paperwork

PIP Coverage

We work directly with your Personal Injury Protection (PIP) insurance to cover your medical expenses.

Florida PIP covers up to $10,000 in medical expenses when treatment begins within 14 days.

Letter of Protection

We accept Letters of Protection from your attorney, allowing you to receive treatment without upfront payment.

Payment is deferred until your case settles, ensuring you get the care you need.

No Upfront Costs

We handle all billing directly with insurance companies and attorneys, so you can focus on recovery.

Our billing team manages all paperwork and authorizations for you.
