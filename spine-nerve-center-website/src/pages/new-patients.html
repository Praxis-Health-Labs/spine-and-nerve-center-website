A Personal Message from Dr. Fura

YOUR FIRST VISIT

What to Expect Step-by-Step

We've designed our process to be as smooth and comfortable as possible

Warm Welcome & Check-In

You'll be greeted by our friendly staff who will help you complete any remaining paperwork and verify your insurance information. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early.

1

Duration: 5-10 minutes

Comprehensive Consultation

Dr. Fura will review your medical history, discuss your symptoms, and listen to your concerns. This is your opportunity to ask questions and share your treatment goals.

2

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Physical Examination

A thorough but gentle examination will help Dr. Fura understand your condition better. We may also review any imaging studies you've brought with you.

3

Duration: 15-20 minutes

Personalized Treatment Plan

Dr. Fura will explain your diagnosis and recommend a customized treatment plan. We'll discuss all options, expected outcomes, and schedule any necessary procedures.

4

Duration: 10-15 minutes

BE PREPARED

What to Bring to Your Appointment

Essential Documents

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance cards
  • Referral (if required)
  • List of current medications

Medical Records

  • Previous medical records
  • X-rays, MRIs, or CT scans
  • Lab results
  • Surgery reports

Payment & Forms

  • Completed patient forms
  • Co-payment (if applicable)
  • List of allergies
  • Emergency contact info

Helpful Tip

Consider bringing a family member or friend for support and to help remember important information discussed during your visit. Write down any questions you have beforehand so you don't forget to ask them.
