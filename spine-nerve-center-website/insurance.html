FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Insurance & Billing FAQs

Please call our office at (813) 741-1071 with your insurance information ready. Our staff will verify your coverage and explain any co-pays or deductibles that may apply. We recommend calling before your appointment to avoid any surprises.

We're actively working on credentialing with major commercial insurance carriers. The process typically takes 3-6 months. We expect to begin accepting several major plans in early 2025. Please check back regularly or call for updates.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for qualifying patients. We understand that healthcare costs can be challenging, and we're committed to working with you to make treatment affordable. Please discuss payment plan options with our billing team.

Please bring your current insurance card, a photo ID, and any referral or authorization forms if required by your plan. For Medicare patients, bring your red, white, and blue Medicare card. If you have supplemental insurance, bring that card as well.

For personal injury cases, we work on a lien basis with your attorney. This means you receive treatment with no upfront costs, and payment is handled through your legal settlement. We'll coordinate directly with your attorney's office for all billing matters.
